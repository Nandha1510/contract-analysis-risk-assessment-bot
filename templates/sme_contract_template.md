# SME-Friendly Service Agreement Template

Date: [Effective Date]

Parties:
- Service Provider: [Name, Address]
- Client: [Name, Address]

1. Scope of Services
- Describe deliverables, acceptance criteria, and milestones.

2. Payment & Invoicing
- Fees: [Amount]
- Payment terms: [e.g., 30 days net]

3. Term & Termination
- Initial term: [duration]
- Termination rights: [mutual termination with notice]

4. Liability
- Cap the liability to [amount or fees paid]

5. Intellectual Property
- Clarify ownership: IP created during engagement shall [be retained by ...]

6. Confidentiality
- Standard NDA clause with limited exceptions.

7. Dispute Resolution
- Prefer mediation then arbitration in [City], India.

Notes for SMEs: Keep clauses short, avoid broad IP assignment, and limit automatic renewal.
